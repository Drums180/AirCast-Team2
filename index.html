      Frequently asked questions

      What types of weather conditions will my flight be flying through?

      The weather conditions along your flight route can vary depending on the time of year and location. It's important to check the forecast before your flight to be prepared for any potential delays or turbulence.

      How accurate is the weather information on your website?

      Our website uses data from reliable sources such as the OpenWeather to provide the most accurate and up-to-date information. However, weather can be unpredictable and it's always a good idea to check multiple sources for the latest forecast.

      Can I change my flight if there is bad weather on the day of my scheduled departure?

      If the weather is causing flight delays or cancellations, airlines may offer the option to change your flight to a later date or provide a refund. It's best to check with your airline directly for the most current information.

      Are there any specific weather conditions that can cause my flight to be diverted?

      Extreme weather conditions such as thunderstorms, icing, and high winds can cause a flight to be diverted to another airport. Pilots will always prioritize the safety of passengers and crew when making a decision to divert.
